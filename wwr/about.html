Rafting Adventures Co.

Our mission is to provide unforgettable experiences on the water, promoting respect for nature and extreme safety with every stroke. Your adventure begins where the shore ends!

History

Founded in the heart of the mountains of Coahuila, Mexico, Rafting Adventure Co. began as a dream of three friends leaded by Francisco Alonso passionate about rivers. Since 1995, we have guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most challenging rapids in the world.
